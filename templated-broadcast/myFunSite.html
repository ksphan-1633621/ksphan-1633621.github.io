Travel together to South America Documented by Morten Rustad.

South America is the foruth largest continent in size, and also the fifth largest in population. South America is located primarily in the Southern hemisphere and it is surrounded by the Atlantic Ocean to the East and Pacific Ocean to the West. Geograohy of South America is dominated by the Andes Mountain Range and the Amazon River.

In the 1500's, Spain and Portugal colonized much of South America. Colonies gained independence in the 1800's with the help of strong leaders like Jose de San MArtin and Simon Bolivar. The majority of South America still speaks Spanish or Portugese as their primary language.
